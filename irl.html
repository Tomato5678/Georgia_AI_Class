Me in Real Life 🌸

I’m someone who finds comfort in quiet moments and simplicity. I enjoy being alone, observing the world from my own little corner—often with a sketchbook or a cup of something warm by my side 🍵🖌️

I love painting, listening to music (all kinds, really), and lately, I’ve been learning how to play the ukulele 🎨🎶. Whether I’m designing, reading, or walking, music is always playing in the background—it's my way of staying grounded.

At home, I share my space with my bunny, Nami 🐇. She's a sweet little soul, and I’m growing a tiny garden on my balcony just for her—some fresh greens and herbs to nibble on 🌿🥬

The sea is my favorite escape. I often walk on the beach early in the morning, when it’s completely quiet and empty, just the sound of waves and my thoughts 🌊☁️

I also love taking photos, playing games, and watching anime—my ways of escaping into other worlds and appreciating beauty in different forms 📷🎮📺

I may not be a fan of crowds, but I deeply enjoy meaningful moments and creative expression. That’s where I feel most like myself 💜
